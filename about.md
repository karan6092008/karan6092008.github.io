Welcome to **TechReview Zero**. Our goal is to provide raw, unsponsored, data-backed hardware benchmarks across CPUs, GPUs, laptops, and mobile devices.

### 📊 Our Benchmarking Methodology
Every device we evaluate goes through an identical set of looped stress runs:
* **Processors:** 10-pass loops on Cinebench R23 to track thermal throttling over time.
* **Graphics Cards:** 3DMark Time Spy synthetic tests paired with raw FPS recordings inside popular game titles.
