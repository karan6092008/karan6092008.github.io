Today we are taking a deep dive into the two most popular processors found in thin-and-light laptops this year. 

### Synthetic Benchmark Scores

* **Cinebench R23 (Multi-Core):** Intel Core Ultra 7 hits 16,200 while AMD Ryzen 7 trails slightly at 14,100.
* **Geekbench 6 (Single-Core):** Intel scores 2,450; AMD scores 2,300.

### Battery & Efficiency Notes
While Intel wins on raw synthetic burst speeds, the **AMD Ryzen 7** dominates on battery efficiency, offering roughly 25% longer battery life under video playback loops.

> **Final Recommendation:** Go Intel if you are rendering video files daily; choose AMD if you need all-day battery life away from a wall outlet.
