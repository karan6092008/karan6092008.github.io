The OnePlus 13R aims to strike the perfect balance between flagship specs and a mid-range price tag. We tested its performance and battery over a full week.

### 📱 Display & Battery Specifications
* **Screen:** 6.78-inch AMOLED, dynamic 120Hz refresh rate
* **Battery:** 5,500 mAh capacity
* **Charging Speed:** 100W Wired SuperVOOC (0 to 100% in 28 minutes)

### 📊 Benchmark Performance (Geekbench 6)
* **Single-Core Score:** 2,150
* **Multi-Core Score:** 6,600

> **The Verdict:** If you care about charging speeds and raw battery endurance, this is the phone to beat. The camera system is average, but the performance per dollar is unmatched.
